Adventures in New York Overview:

Kick off your Statue of Liberty and Ellis Island adventure with a scenic ferry ride from Battery Park in Lower Manhattan. The ferry departs frequently throughout the day, providing stunning views of New York Harbor as you journey toward the Statue of Liberty on Liberty Island. Afterward, visit the Ellis Island National Museum of Immigration, where you can learn about the rich history of American immigration.

What to Wear:

To enjoy your adventure comfortably, dress in layers as conditions on the water can be cool. Recommended items include: Comfortable walking shoes for exploring the islands A light jacket or coat, especially in spring or fall A hat and sunglasses for sun protection Consider bringing binoculars and a camera to capture the sights and the New York City skyline.

Reservations:

To secure tickets, visit the official Statue Cruises website, the exclusive provider of ferry tickets to the islands. You can choose from general admission, pedestal, or crown access. For more information, call 877-LADY-TIX or check for online ticket availability.
